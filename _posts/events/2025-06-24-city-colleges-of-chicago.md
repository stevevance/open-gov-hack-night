[The City Colleges of Chicago](https://coe.ccc.edu/tech/) can help students prepare to succeed in a variety of tech fields with instructors that are industry leaders and cutting-edge curriculum, which is constantly evolving to meet the needs of employers. The opportunities and support provided in and out of the classroom allow students to launch careers or level-up their skills in tech with:

- Flexible schedules
- Short-term, career-focused certifications
- Guaranteed-transfer pathways to four-year institutions
- Apprenticeships
- A learning community, and more

City Colleges helps students enter and evolve in a tech career—no matter where they start. With classes offering college credit, industry certifications, and professional development, it’s an affordable and life-changing tech education. City Colleges partners with some of the biggest names in tech, ensuring that CCC students learn relevant skills, and that employers have direct access to top talent. Together, we create customized training programs, internships, and apprenticeships, as well as fund programs that benefit both students and employers.
